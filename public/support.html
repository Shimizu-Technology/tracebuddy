Support

TraceBuddy helps kids and parents use a phone or iPad for camera-over-paper tracing and on-screen coloring practice.

Getting started

  • Pick a built-in drawing, type a custom word, or choose a local image.
  • Use Practice to trace or color directly on the device.
  • Use Camera to place the tracing guide over real paper.
  • Images, camera video, custom words, and drawings stay on the device.

Camera tips

  • Use good lighting and a stable phone or tablet stand.
  • Increase or decrease guide opacity so the paper remains visible.
  • Lock the overlay once it is aligned.
